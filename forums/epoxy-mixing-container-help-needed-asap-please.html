I used red Dixie cups and standard laminating mixing containers.

I cannot find any specific details on which plastic are used in these.

I believe that the bulk of your problems arise when you factor in Xylene as a thinning agent for the epoxy...as this can break down certain kinds of plastic.

Thinking aloud...that might be a good test...does Xylene melt the container you are trying to use...if yes, find another container (or don't use Xylene).
