well...the place is called sandspit, and its actually the santa barbara harbor. the harbor kinda kinks at the end and theres a "sand spit" that sits on the outside of the kink...and there you have it, huge backwash and super hollow waves!

its empty unless the swell is an exact northwest, and it better be a big swell at that...so it breaks 4-5 times a year, and EVERYONE from far and wide comes to fill it up...but it seems like when its really firing, its all groms out there...groms and pro's, a few old guys...and me taking pics!

okay...ive blabbed enough..its a cool spot, but really crowded when it fires.
