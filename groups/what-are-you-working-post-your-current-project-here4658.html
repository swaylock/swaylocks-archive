G'day HCS, Very observant of you there mate. Yes indeed my rails are wrap around at 3mm just like the rest of the board. In this way I remain true to the original shape/shaper. Thanks for your kind words Sir. It's just the 'weight' question gets  me. If I can build a surfboard that weighs 5% more yet floats 10% better the weight argument needs a re-sleeving keeping in mind I'm not building for Kelly or Joel [yet] and if I were.. I'd be reducing the board's profile by 15 or 20%. 

In any case, to placate the market place and their facinating whims and fancifull desires I'm experimenting with lighter weighted wood in the name of Maple veneer at 1.2mm at half the weight of Cedar @ 3.2mm.  This should reduce the overall weight by some 700 gram.  What's that in the old scale?
