Hey There are no absolutes for glassing any size board. Depends what you are looking for. Hi performance funboarding, go light. Durable and long lasting, go double bottom, 2.5 top. Most, not all funboards are glassed with 5 or 6 oz. cloths. If you fall, or are still learning, go heavy.
