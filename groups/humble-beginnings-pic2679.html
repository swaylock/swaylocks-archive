Home Depot color: Bear..."Isle of Capri" I think it was called. It is very soothing and a good contrast also. I usually only shape one a day so that I totally focus on that one. I am not one to fuss unecessarily. Sand to 220 and go! Note: The Halogen bulbs in my overhead don't seem to affect the UV catalized resin. Krokus
