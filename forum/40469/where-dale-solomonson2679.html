I ordered my mat in 2006!

Inquires by phone messages (no one ever answers) and by emails were not answered, or even acknowledged.  A company policy it seems...

Despite being extremely dissatisfied with Dale's purposeful avoidance of any semblance of professionalism, or even common courtesy, when I heard he that he had a heart attack I emailed him offering to allow him to consider my $350 payment for a raft as donation to a fellow waterman if he was financially desperate (although I certainly do not have an extra $350 laying around~I live on $1K/Mo. social security~try that!).

Eighteen months or so back Dale did email me to refresh his recollection of our discussions during the process of ordering my mat (height, weight, expected type wave, etc.  He told me that he would get to my build "soon".  NOT.

About six months ago, just before I qualified for social security I emailed Dale and BEGGED for my money back because I was BROKE, couch-surfing, and dumpster diving. NO RESPONSE~BIG SURPRISE.

Dale may be a good guy, but from my perspective as an advance paid customer he has been a complete failure as a competent and trustworthy businessman.

How many mats do you think he has built and delivered since 2006? I can understand friends and known riders getting prompt builds, but there is absolutely no excuse for taking my money and ignoring me for YEARS. I bet there is no other individual that has waited SIX YEARS.

A quality hand built product.  I'll back up John's warranty as a satisfied customer of one of Dale Solomonson's Neumatic surfmats.
