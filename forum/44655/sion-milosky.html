I was Papa's glasser for years til he went psyco one day and I told him that if we were to stay friends then we had to stop doing business together. I had heard that McDuff moved to Canada but then moved to Colorado but I also heard he came back to Kauai or wanted to but Chuck's didn't have a job or him and of course he had lost his house on the river by then.
