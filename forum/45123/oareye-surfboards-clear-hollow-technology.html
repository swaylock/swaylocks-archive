When I worked for Gary Linden, I saw a board come in from South Africa. It was solid clear plastic. It had a spine with ribs out to the rail. Totally clear two piece top and bottom with a what looked like a hot shrink-wrap tape to secure the seam on the rail. It also had solid plastic section for the FCS plugs. It was really light and totally clear. Never saw another one. It looked similar to the one posted here but was all plastic, nothing more.
