My name is Troy Mize. I lived in Pearl City from 1960 to 64. I went to Highlands Intermidiate school 7th,8th and 9th grades. I was the only Haole in my class for 3 years there. Eric "Tiger" Espere was in my 8th and 9th grade class. He was a super good guy to me and saved me from a couple of beatings! I surfed with him at number 3's and Makaha a few times. He was surfing on a board about 7'6inches in 63 and way way ahead of his time. I also started surfing at Barbers Point in 62 along side of Rick and Kevin Johns. My brothers Lance and Chris surfed Ewa Beach area for years with Chris,Steve and Mark Gardner. Mark liked to take his shorts off and surf naked, which got him in trouble at times with the Officers at O. beach.

 Kevin Johns was a kind person and was always friendly. He was a good Goofy foot! I hope he's surfing right now at a perfect left in Heaven, with his brother Rick! Who was killed surfing between Chuns Reef and Laniakea in 64. I hope they're together with Chris and Mark Gardner having fun surfing forever. Mark Gardner stayed with us at my fathers house for over a year, then he camped out in our back yard for a year, always cooking his brown rice late at night. Troy Mize p.s. I went to Radford from 63 to 64 If anyone remembers let me know
