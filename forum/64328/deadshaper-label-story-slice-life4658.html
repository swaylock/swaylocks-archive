sharkcountry wrote:

Hi Bruce I read the story on the Shaper's Tree about you several years ago.

One thing I noticed about the new logo is that the hat has the stoker V machine logo on it. Does that make it an endorsement for those boards which then requires the licensing fee? Just wondering. Best of luck with the new brand. Be cool if you had it in a colorful version like the old Country Surfboards logo.

Answer: Excellent question.  When Rob drew the original artwork the payment was a custom Stoker V Machine from me. I'll dig up the laminate we produced for the entire bottom of his board that I glassed onto it for the Stokefest held at David Zeimer's house in Santa Barbara.  Since I decided to no longer offer the logo due to a dispute over some unauthorized boards being made contrary to what the contract outlined and was agreed upon, the logo is being removed and replaced with my bulls eye logo which is a public domain symbol that I have used in different sizes, shapes, and forms since the 70's.  

Although the "Stoker V Machine" label drawn by Thomas Campbell and given to Randall Rostoker in 1999 as a birthday gift has never been trademarked, I still respect that the logo is his and has a documented history of being put on different surfboard shapes he prefers to ride.  Those surfboards do not represent any "Intellectual Property" as they are very similar to many surfboards that have been made by numerous shapers for the past 40+ years: sometimes referred to as "the 40 year bubble".  

No surfboard has ever been patented for unique tail, rail, deck, bottom, or outline shapes in the history of surfboards.  The only patentable designs I know of involved hardware surrounding a shape; for example, the latches used for the Morey Pope Bi & Tri-Sect, perhaps the Campbell Brothers Bonzer design with Venturi Concave and adjacent runner fins, and Hoyle & Schweitzer's  Universal Joint that connected mast to board on their "Windsurfer". 

P.S.  Yes, we are going to do a colorized version and I used the "Country Surfboards" example in my discussion for producing it. Man, you read my mind or my office is tapped..... lol.
