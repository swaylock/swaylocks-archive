On Saturday, October 14, from 6 Am until 1 PM, the Vintage Surfboard Collectors Club will be hosting their annual SHACC parking lot meet. Then later that same day, Robb Havassy will be doing a book signing/art and photo exhibit reception for his book, Surf Story 2. This will also be a tribute to recently departed art maven and all around amazing person, Greg Escalante who left us all too soon. This is an all ages, free event.
