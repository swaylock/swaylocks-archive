Yew, its 5'4" by 21" by 2 5/8" and around 32-33 ltrs

Low rocker of 3.5 in the nose and 2 in the tail. Slight single to double that sort of ends in a V out the tail. The quad set up is based on the Hanalie quad placements rather than the Mackee quads. I put a box in so i can run it as a single fin. I call it the "Sunny Comfort", the comfy go to board for our sunny coast waves when its not to serious and a bit guttless. Hopefully it grovells and flies in a various range of conditions. - See more at: http://www.swaylocks.com/forums/build-thread-my-4th-board-start-to-finis...
