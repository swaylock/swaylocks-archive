I attempted to contact Scarfini (ZA) a few times back in 2007 and 2008. Emailed through the address on the website, no one ever replied, called the number, nobody ever answered ( sounded like a "ringing" tone ).

I was interested in importing them into Australia as I could find no agent here at the time. Then suddenly in 2009 this Afrikanner expat sets up shop as Scarfini agent in my home town. Needless to say... he's getting no love from me.

BTW... did anyone else ( board makers etc. ) get the scary letter from Surf Hardware in January regarding the patents referred to in this post? They basically say that *someone* has been importing non-FCS plugs and putting them in boards for sale in Australia. They "discovered" a "close to exact copy of the FCS G5 fin and the smoke coloured X2 plug". The letter then went on to say that "This supplier has agreed to cease selling this product to avoid further action being taken against them".

Sounds like they are pretty serious about enforcing their IP rights.
