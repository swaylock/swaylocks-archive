808 shapes (not verified)

Ben Franklin craft stores in Hawaii carry some stuff that i've used. Not sure if other craft stores in other areas carry the same stuff. It's 18 inches wide by 50 ft. roll. Its called Oriental Rice Paper for sumi, calligraphy, water color and sketching. the item # on the label is P-905 and made by Loew and Cornell out of Jersey. very good results with this stuff, just trim close to your printed graphics when using on dark colored boards, very slight milky color over the darker air brush jobs in the clear (white) part of the lam. Aloha 808 shapes
