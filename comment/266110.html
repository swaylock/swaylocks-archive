The group camp event is still on! Fire restrictions are in effect at this time.

CYNTHIA CRISSMAN at WARVEL SURF CORES (http://www.warvelsurf.com/) has generously offered a WMD blank for the raffle. They are also providing a finished demo board.

KEN EBERT at SEGWAY COMPOSITES (http://www.segwaycomposites.com/) has informed me that he will be providing a computer pre-shaped SUP blank.

Many thanks go out to Ken and Cynthia for their generous contributions! I know they've both been busy but it's heartening to know that they're finding time to step up and help out the little guys. Ken is distributing Warvel blanks on the west coast so it's definitely worth taking a look at their product.

Ken has a fantastic selection of EPS paddleboard blank designs available along with some great surfboard blank designs. Greg Loehr designed many of the blanks including some with parabolic stringer configurations. Ken is also offering machine shaping and contract glassing services.

With the Warvel MDI polyurethane in his catalog, he's covering all the bases.

There are still a few spots left so if you decide you can make it, please let me know ASAP. THANKS!!!

EDIT - Ken surprised me with two shortboard pre-shaped blanks along with the pre-shaped SUP blank (on top of block) to the raffle. Cynthia donated the WMD (Warvel Multi Density) with 5lb polyurethane rails and EPS core blank along with a finished Warvel demo board. Sorry, the two blanks (11' X 31" X 5") inside the big block are taken.

Thanks again to the generous sponsors!
