Barry (not verified)

Very likely your board was finished shaped by Jim Phillips. Tommy Maus was not on board until a few years later. Jim Phillips had a true appreciation for Dale's board designs and his style of shaping and felt a genuine responsibility to faithfully reproduce them. We were very fortunate to have had his assistance. I did not look your board up in my files but I did take a look at the Malibu's site. Your board is a "Modern Classic." It is slimmer through the hips than a "New Traditional" and has a slightly fuller nose and trim area. If you have any other questions, please send an e-mail and I will get back to you as quickly as I am able. Regards, Barry Jones (Formerly, Surfboards by Velzy)
