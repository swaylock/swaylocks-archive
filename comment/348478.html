Upcoming 2010 Hollow Surfboard Workshops

Seattle, Washington USA

There will be a Five day - Build Your Own Board workshop in Mukilteo, (near Seattle) on Feb. 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 2010…Sorry for the somewhat short notice, but this one is coming together pretty quick…

This workshop have up to ten participants build their own boards to the point they will be ready for glassing. If Time allows we might get started glassing, it’s up to you…

Tuition will be $700 per person, with materials extra, typically about $45 per foot of board length...

Again, the "How To" CD and your choice of a frame kit will be included in the cost of the class...The board choices ( 5’10” Fish, 7’4” Hybrid, 9’0” Longboard and maybe one or two others)

A reservation fee of $100 will be required.I’ll set up a payPal link in the next week…

Please decide soon, and let me know via e-mail one way or another, because I have a lot to plan for and as much notice as possible is appreciated…This will be the only class in Washington State this year...

Thanks and I hope to have you part of this class…

Look for workshops this year in:

Australia - Albany WA October 4,5,6,7,8

Australia - Sapphire Coast NSW October 20,21,22,23,24

Japan - Chiba September 20,21,22,23,24

Spain in Spring or Summer

France in late April

Dates to be announced when more is known...Stay tuned...
